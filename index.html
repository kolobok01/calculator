Why Banks Win in Tokenized Finance

DeFi infrastructure meets regulatory moat — a strategic opportunity

Deposits Stay on Your Balance Sheet

Unlike stablecoins (USDC, USDT), WFUSD is a bank deposit liability. Funds remain in your custody, earning NIM. You're not sending float to Circle or Tether.

New Fee Revenue on Existing Infrastructure

Tokenization creates fee opportunities at every touchpoint: minting (0.03%), FX (0.75%), settlement (£15), custody (0.10%), lending (4-8% APR). Stack revenue on flows you already process.

Regulatory Moat vs Crypto Native Players

You already have the banking license, KYC infrastructure, and regulatory relationships. DeFi protocols can't offer FDIC-insured deposit tokens. This is your competitive advantage.

40% Cost Reduction vs SWIFT/Correspondent

Cross-border settlement drops from T+2 to T+0. Eliminate nostro pre-funding requirements. Smart contracts replace manual reconciliation. Direct P&L impact.
